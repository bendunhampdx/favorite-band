Solana Rowe, known as SZA, is an American R&B singer and songwriter. She is signed with Top Dawg Entertainment (TDE), through which she released her debut studio album, "ctrl" in 2017. It debuted at number three on US Billboard and was eventually nominated for four Grammys.

Albums

SZA has one studio album thus far, 'ctrl.'
